my most representative projects from recent years


Università degli Studi di Milano

Research Project: “Big Data and Early Archives (Big-DEA). Measuring Settlement Dynamics and Environmental Exploitation in the Ebla Region during the 3rd Millennium BC: Archaeological Record, Cuneiform Texts, and Remote Sensing (PRIN 2017)”
https://orienteantico.unimi.it/en/big-dea/
Principal Tasks:
• Database and GIS Management: Created, managed and maintained the project's database and GIS systems.
• Remote sensing location: Utilized remote sensing techniques to identify prehistoric Bronze Age archaeological sites.
• Territorial and spatial analysis: Conducted detailed analyses to reconstruct ancient landscapes.
• Webgis development: Developed a WebGIS platform for efficient data visualization, export, and sharing..


Università degli Studi di Milano | Natioanl Museum of Denmark

Research project: “Ordinary Lives in Extraordinary Times: A New View of the Earliest Urban Societies in Bronze Age Syria”
https://orienteantico.unimi.it/en/hama/
Principal Tasks:
• Digitalization of Plans: Converted archaeological excavation plans into digital formats
• Online Database Creation: Developed an interactive, browser-based archaeological database integrated with a GIS platform to streamline data management.
• Data management and spatial analyses: Managed and analyzed large datasets within a GIS environment to derive comprehensive research insights.
• WebGIS Development: Created a webGIS solution that enables efficient data consultation, export, and sharing among project stakeholders.
• 3D reconstructions: Produced detailed 3D models to visualize and reconstruct ancient buildings and urban landscapes.


Bologna region

Aerophotogrammetric survey of ford areas along watercourses for the design of the "Ciclovia del Sole," delivering outputs that included point clouds, digital terrain models (DTM), and floor plans generated in a GIS environment.


Parma region

Drone-based photogrammetric surveys of various residential buildings for the Superbonus 110% design, in collaboration with Eco Care Building. Additionally, CAD renderings of the elevations and floor plans were produced.

Introd (AO)

Drone-based photogrammetric survey of the old bridge in Introd, carried out as part of Project No. 4839 “Parcours des patrimoines, de passages en châteaux – P.P.P.C.”, as outlined in the Integrated Territorial Plan (P.I.Ter.) “Parcours. Un patrimoine, une identité, des parcours partagés”, within the Interreg V-A Italy-France ALCOTRA 2014/2020 cross-border cooperation program, located in the Municipality of Introd (AO).
Survey aimed not only at preparing educational panels but also at drafting a future restoration project. Therefore, after the field survey activities, an interpretative stratigraphic analysis of the structure was conducted at a reproduction scale of 1:20 (Sheets 2–7), with particular attention to the construction materials, execution techniques, and any traces left on the abutment walls (e.g., pier holes, inserts, etc.) and, along the corresponding face of these, on the banks; as well as the identification of any signs of preparatory work (e.g., rock cuts, holes, etc.) and of the original scaffolding system installed at the time of the structure’s construction.



Rieti (RI)

Planimetric and altimetric topographic survey using GNSS (GPS) and a Total Station of an 18 km section of SS 79 Ternana near Rieti for a road barrier rehabilitation project.


Sasso Marconi (BO)

Planimetric and altimetric topographic survey using GNSS (GPS) and a Total Station of a section of road and stream near Sasso Marconi (BO) for a road deviation project and bridge design.
